Tahidi High school

Welcome to Tahidi High School, where excellence and opportunity converge. Our curriculum spans Mathematics, Sciences, Languages, Humanities, and Arts, preparing students for future success. Located just 1km past the Main entrance to the Kenyatta International Airport in Nairobi, our school offers modern facilities including classrooms, science labs, a library, and sports fields. Alongside academics, we emphasize co-curricular activities like sports, clubs, music, drama, debates, and community service to foster well-rounded individuals. Our dedicated staff are committed to guiding students towards academic achievement and personal growth.

HISTORY

Tahidi High was founded in 1965 by a group of visionary educators with a mission to provide a transformative educational experience for the youth in our community. From its inception, the school aimed to create a nurturing environment that would not only foster academic excellence but also cultivate character, leadership, and a sense of civic responsibility among its students. In its early years, Tahidi High faced challenges and obstacles, but through perseverance and dedication, it emerged as a beacon of educational excellence. The school quickly gained a reputation for its innovative teaching methods, rigorous academic curriculum, and commitment to holistic student development. Over the decades, Tahidi High has continued to evolve and adapt to the changing needs of its students and the community. It has expanded its academic offerings, developed strong partnerships with local organizations, and implemented cutting-edge technology to enhance the learning experience. Today, Tahidi High stands as a proud institution with a rich history of producing graduates who excel in various fields, from academia to the arts, from business to public service. The school's alumni network spans the globe, with graduates making their mark in diverse industries and making significant contributions to society. As we celebrate our past achievements and look towards the future, Tahidi High remains committed to providing a world-class education that prepares students to meet the challenges of tomorrow with confidence, integrity, and compassion.
